How to Book Your Plot in Saffron City, Islamabad?

Booking your plot in Saffron City Islamabad is a simple and straightforward process designed to make your investment easy and hassle-free. Follow these easy steps to secure your place in this premium housing society:

1) Choose Your Plot

Select your preferred plot size from available options like 5 Marla, 10 Marla, 1 Kanal, or commercial plots.

2) Contact Our Sales Team

Get in touch with our authorized sales representatives for current availability, prices, and payment plans.

3) Submit Required Documents

Provide your CNIC copy, passport-sized photos, and any other required documents to start the booking process.

4) Pay Booking Amount

Make the initial booking payment through bank transfer, cheque, or online payment as guided by our sales team.

5) Receive Allotment Letter

Once the payment and documents are verified, you will receive an official allotment letter confirming your plot.

6) Complete Remaining Payments

Pay the remaining amount according to the agreed payment plan to complete your purchase.
